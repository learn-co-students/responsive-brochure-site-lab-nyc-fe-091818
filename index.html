SOMETIMES I PAINT IN A DICTIONARY

ABOUT

At a time in my life when it was hard to justify buying expensive watercolor paper, I found a dictionary on the street. It turned out to be my neverending sketchbook, a cool place where I can mess up and try things out because I'll never run out of pages. Dictionaries have a lot of pages.
